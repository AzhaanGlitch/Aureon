# Aureon

Aureon is a comprehensive financial management application built with React and Vite. It helps users take control of their finances through smart budgeting, goal tracking, bill management, and AI-powered insights.

## Features

### Dashboard
The central hub for your financial overview.
* Financial Snapshot: Real-time view of your current financial status.
* AI Insights: Automated analysis of your spending habits and financial health.
* Spending Charts: Visual representations of your expenses over time.
* Quick Actions: Easily add transactions and access key features.

### Budget Management
Advanced tools to keep your spending on track.
* Real-time Tracking: Monitor your total budget, amount spent, and remaining balance.
* Category Breakdown: Detailed view of spending across different categories with status indicators.
* AI Budget Optimizer: Intelligent suggestions to adjust budgets based on actual spending patterns.

### Financial Goals
Set and achieve your financial targets.
* Progress Tracking: Visual progress bars for each goal.
* Timeline Analysis: Estimates on deadlines and required monthly contributions.
* Status Indicators: Know immediately if you are ahead, behind, or on track with your saving goals.

### Bills & Subscriptions
Manage recurring expenses efficiently.
* Bill Calendar: Track upcoming due dates to avoid late fees.
* Subscription Manager: Monitor active subscriptions and identify unused services.
* Savings Analyzer: AI-driven detection of potential savings from unused subscriptions.

### AI Assistant
An interactive financial companion.
* Chat Interface: Ask questions about your finances in natural language.
* Smart Analysis: Get instant answers about spending habits, budget status, and expense categorization.

## Tech Stack

* Framework: React
* Build Tool: Vite
* Styling: Tailwind CSS
* Routing: React Router DOM
* Icons: Lucide React
